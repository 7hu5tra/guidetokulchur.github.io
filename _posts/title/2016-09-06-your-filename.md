## from Barbara Ruth's
### At 69

	scarlet lipstick smears
    on the pad
    of my foot
    first time except for
    when I was a
    baby
    maybe
    quite likely
    my mother, my aunties,
    my grandmas kissed my soles
    with their painted mouths
    the way I`ve busied baby toes,
    with my naked lips.
